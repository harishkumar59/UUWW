About the Exam

What is UUWW?

The Unified Undergraduate Written Examination (UUWW) is a national-level entrance test conducted for admission to B.Tech / B.E. programs in engineering and technology colleges across India.

UUWW is recognized by over 4,200 institutions and serves as the single window entrance for students who have cleared their Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM).

The examination tests candidates on their aptitude, reasoning, and subject knowledge across core science and mathematics domains.

  • ✅ National Level Examination
  • ✅ Recognized by NBTE & AICTE Approved Colleges
  • ✅ Computer Based Test (CBT) Mode
  • ✅ Results Valid for 1 Academic Year

Who Can Apply

Eligibility Criteria

01

Educational Qualification

Must have passed Class 12 (10+2) or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects from a recognized board.

02

Minimum Marks

General category: 60% aggregate in PCM. OBC: 55%. SC/ST/PwD: 50% aggregate marks.

03

Age Limit

Minimum age: 17 years as on October 1, 2026. No upper age limit for most participating institutions.

04

Attempts

No restriction on the number of attempts. Candidates appearing in Class 12 final exam in 2026 are also eligible to apply.
